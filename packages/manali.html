Destination: Manali

Itinerary

Day 1: Train departure at from Ahmedabad Sabarmati Railway Station, with a third AC reservation, headed for Chandigarh

Day 2: Train arrives in Chandigarh at, followed by a bus ride to Manali. A two-hour break is taken for river rafting at Kullu. Hotel check-in at the Manali River Bank. See the Hidimba Temple in the evening, then have dinner and an enlightening campfire discussion about the plans for the next six days.

Day 3: Yoga and pranayama in the morning, brunch, a trip to the Manu Temple, a trip to Oxygen Park, a walk down Mall Road, a hotel lunch followed by a river crossing, supper, and a campfire in the evening.

Day 4: Morning exercise, breakfast, trekking to Jogini fall, Visit to Vashisht Ashram, bathing in hot springs, afternoon return to the hotel for lunch, shopping on Mall Road, and campfire in the evening.

Day 5: Morning exercise, have a blast in the snow at Atal Tunnel, children's water park and waterfall in Shishu and then head over to Jispa camping grounds for a night stay in tents surrounded by mountains. Dinner by the river and a DJ party.

Day 6: Morning breakfast, Take a ride down Leh Ladakh national highway towards Solang Valley with a beautiful mesmerizing mountain view for about 100 km. Enjoy activities at Solang Valley such as- horse riding, zorbing, Paragliding, Gondola and evening campfire or DJ party.

Day 7: Start the day with a workout, grab breakfast, and if we missed it on the first day, hit up the Kullu-Manali rafting. Later in the day, hop on a bus headed for Chandigarh and get on a Train to way back to Ahmedabad.

Day 8: Return to Ahmedabad by late evening.

Accommodation: Hotel Riverbank

Indulge in the comfort of our exclusive hotel with breathtaking views, top-notch amenities, and an extra-ordinary service.
